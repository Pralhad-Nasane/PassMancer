# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 27, 2025**

PassMancer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.


## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

PassMancer works entirely offline. No data is sent to any server.

## What We Store Locally

The app stores the following data **only on your device**:

| Data | Purpose | Storage Duration |
|------|---------|------------------|
| Password History | View recently generated passwords | Auto-deleted after 24 hours |
| App Settings | Your preferences (screenshot protection, etc.) | Until you clear app data |

This data never leaves your device.

## Permissions

PassMancer requires minimal permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Clipboard | Copy generated passwords |

We do not request:
- Internet access
- Location
- Camera
- Contacts
- Storage access to external files

## Third-Party Services

PassMancer does not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Any third-party SDKs that collect data


## Data Security

- All data stays on your device
- Password history is stored using DataStore
- Optional screenshot protection blocks screen capture

## Children's Privacy

PassMancer is safe for all ages. We do not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
